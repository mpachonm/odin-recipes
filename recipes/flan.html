Flan

Description

Flan is a popular Spanish dessert and different regions make different versions. It is essentially a custard dessert made by combining eggs, milk, sugar (or condensed milk), and vanilla and it is baked in a water bath similar to the way a Cheesecake is baked.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • A can of sweetened condensed milk
  • A can of evaporated milk
  • A tablespoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Melt the sugar on the stove, then pour it into a round baking dish.
  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl, then stir in the remaining ingredients.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the baking dish. Cover with foil
  4. Bake for an hour in the preheated oven.
